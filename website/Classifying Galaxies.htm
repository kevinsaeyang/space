Barred Spiral VS Spiral Elliptical VS Spiral Peculiar VS Everything else

Similar in nature, spiral and barred spiral both have noticible winding arms. The way to tell the difference between the two is that one has a psychical bar running through the middle. Like the name, barred spirals(the image to the left) are the galaxies that have the bar running through them that connects to that arms. On the other hand, spiral galaxies(image on the right) just have the unwinding arms.
