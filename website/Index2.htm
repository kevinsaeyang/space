Types of Galaxies

In simple terms, a galaxy is a huge collections of stars, dust and gas. With this there are 4 main types of galaxies:

Spiral

Spiral galaxies get their name from the winding shape that they have.

These "spirals" are known as arms.

Barred Spiral

Similar to spiral galaxies, Barred spirals share the winding arms

but they have a bar running through the center.

The bar itself is composed of stars.

Elliptical

Elliptical galaxies are the most common type of galaxies.

They have a rounded, streached out shape shape,

similar to an ellipsve.

Peculiar

Peculair galaxies are the odd one out of the group.

They can be classified by the asymmetrical shape that they have.

Other types

Besides the main four their are two other types of galaxies:

Irregular galaxy

similar to peculiar, irregular galaxies are

they have no distinct shape to them

Lenticular galaxy

Lenticular galaxies are like elliptical galaxies

but denser, making the galaxy look like a lense
